Introduction

This page is mostly about my contributions to Namecoin.

My Start

My contributions to Namecoin started when I began working on adding Namecoin currency support to Armory. I had a question that I asked some Namecoin developers. From there I stuck around and worked on some other Namecoin projects.

The Projects

My Armory repository ended up getting forked by the official Namecoin GitHub account. I also worked (or am working) on some stuff related to NMControl, most notably Python 3 support and an Android version called NMControl for Android. I am also part of the group looking into build automation and reproducibility for Namecoin software.
